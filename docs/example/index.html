About Us

We are a group of software engineering university students who are passionate about games and game development! This is our 4th year capstone project which aims to help empower other game developers! We are looking to apply what we've learnt in our 4 years of university to our interest in games! Some of the algorithms and concepts we applied from our lectures include but are not limited to: Djikstra's Algorithm, Grammar Rules, Perlin Noise, Bilinear Interpolation, GPU shaders, etc.

If you have any questions or feature requests, we are more than happy to help!
